About Us

Welcome to CNY Gym Centre of DeWitt and Clay, NY!

We offer gymnastics classes for boys and girls ages 6 months to 18 years. We coach competitive gymnastics team levels 2 - 10 and Xcel. CNY Gym Centre also offers Funtastic birthday parties, school vacation and summer camps,ninja classes, back handspring and tumbling clinics. We offer month-to-month tuition, providing convenient scheduling. A safe gymnastics environment is our priority, with team coaches who are trained and accredited, with background checks, and ongoing training for certification. Our staff includes knowledgeable ex-gymnasts. Be sure to check out our Ninja Warrior Training, gymnastics-influenced training for boys and girls, offered only at our ShoppingTown location.

Voted the Best Gymnastics Program in Syracuse and Central NY!
